Your Alpha Global Youth Network (YAGYN)

Your Alpha Global Youth Network (YAGYN) is a youth movement under Build Africa Initiative focused on empowering young people through mentorship, skills training, and self-reliance.

Objectives

  • Promote education and lifelong learning
  • Develop character and leadership
  • Encourage self-reliance and entrepreneurship
  • Support vulnerable youth
