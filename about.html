About Us

Build Africa Initiative is a humanitarian organization working to empower youth and vulnerable communities through education, skills development, mentorship, and sustainable projects.

Our Vision

A self-reliant and empowered generation of young people transforming Africa.

Our Mission

To equip young people and communities with knowledge, practical skills, and opportunities that promote dignity, self-reliance, and development.
